        foodbank > Immovable invisible=true
        wall > Immovable color=BLACK img=wall
        goal > Door color=GREEN img=goal

        bubble > Chaser stype=avatar invisible=True hidden=True
        bubblehole > SpawnPoint stype=bubble cooldown=15 invisible=True hidden=True

        food > Resource img=carcass img=carcass

        food avatar > addHealthPoints value=15
        food avatar > killSprite
        avatar foodbank > addHealthPoints value=15
        foodbank avatar > killSprite scoreChange=40

        goal avatar  > killSprite scoreChange=100
        avatar wall  > stepBack

        avatar bubble > subtractHealthPoints
        bubble avatar > killSprite

    TerminationSet
        SpriteCounter stype=goal   limit=0 win=True
        SpriteCounter stype=avatar limit=0 win=False

    LevelMapping
        G > goal
        A > avatar food
        f > foodbank food food food
        X > bubblehole wall
