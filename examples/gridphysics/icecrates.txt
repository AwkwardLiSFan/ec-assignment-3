#IceCrates: http://www.puzzlescript.net/play.html?p=6877049

BasicGame
    SpriteSet
        avatar > OrientedAvatar
        hole   > Immovable color=DARKBLUE img=hole
        ground > Immovable img=water
        box    > Passive img=box
        boxin  > Passive img=city
        boxmovin > Missile img=hell
    LevelMapping
        A > avatar
        0 > hole
        B > box
    InteractionSet
        boxmovin wall > transformTo stype=box 
        boxmovin box > transformTo stype=box

        avatar wall > stepBack
        box avatar  > transformTo stype=boxmovin
        boxmovin avatar > attractGaze
        avatar box > stepBack
        avatar boxmovin > stepBack
        box wall > undoAll
        box box > undoAll

    TerminationSet
        SpriteCounter stype=avatar    limit=0 win=False
